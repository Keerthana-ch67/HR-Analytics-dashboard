An end-to-end HR Analytics dashboard focuses on analyzing employee data to monitor workforce performance, attrition trends and key HR metrics. enables HR teams and management to track employee retention, departmental performance to support strategic decision-making.
# Key Highlights:

1. Attrition Analysis: Identified high‑risk departments and job roles by tracking turnover patterns.
2. Employee Engagement: Analyzed survey data to highlight drivers of satisfaction and retention.
3. Interactive Dashboard: Developed executive‑ready visuals with drill‑down features for HR managers.
   
# Business Objectives

Monitor total workforce distribution.

Measure attrition and retrenchment rate.

Track employees due for promotion.

Analyze gender diversity and job levels.

Evaluate department-wise workforce risk.

Identify overtime and distance-related workforce patterns.
